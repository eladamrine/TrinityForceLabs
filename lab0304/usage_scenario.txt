Use Case UC-2: BookCars

Brief Description: After a search, the customer can book the car(s) they want to rent. 

Initating Actor - Customer

Actor's goal - To book car(s) that the customer wants to rent. 

Participating actors - QBEI Insurances, External CC Validator. 

Flow of Events for the Main Success Scenario:

->  1. Customer searches for cars. 
<-  2. The system displays results.
->  3. Customer selects a car. 
<-  4. System prompts customer for personal and payment information. 
->  5. Customer inputs information. 
<-  6. System validates information. 
<-  7. System sends customer a confirmation email.
